About STOdrones

The company is providing aerial photography and film services. We are fully licensed to operate on the territory of the Czech Republic. The license has been granted by the Czech Civil Aviation Authority.

Our skilled pilot has passed the CAA-approved exams, ﬂight assessments, and hold the established industry flight operational license for multi-rotor aircraft under 7kg.

Our experienced pilots are capable of flying our agile drones close to difficult locations, day or night while maintaining the highest standards in safety and operation.

Drone Photography Services are pioneering commercial drone photography flying up to 300m, turning new ideas for aerial imagery into stunning reality.
