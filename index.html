About STOdrones

The company is providing aerial photography and film services. We are fully licensed to operate on the territory of the Czech Republic. The license has been granted by the Czech Civil Aviation Authority. Drone Photography Services produce amazing quality aerial drone photography and aerial filming in Cornwall, the South West and throughout the UK using the latest GPS enabled multi-rotor UAV drone quadcopters . Drone Photography Services has Civil Aviation Authority (CAA) “Permission for Commercial Operations” PfCO which means we are a licensed commercial operator of UAV’s (unmanned aerial vehicles) drones in the U.K for day and night work. All of our skilled pilots have passed the CAA-approved exams, ﬂight assessments, and hold the established industry Remote Pilot Qualiﬁcation (RPQ-s) for multi-rotor aircraft under 7kg. Our experienced pilots are capable of flying our agile drones close to difficult locations, day or night while maintaining the highest standards in safety and operation. Drone Photography Services are pioneering commercial drone photography flying up to 400ft, turning new ideas for aerial imagery into stunning reality.

Equipment

Real Estate (Private & Commercial)

We produce dramatic aerial property photography and videos for residential estate agents. Aerial videos and photographs capture the scale of the property, land, gardens and surrounding, not possible with ground-based photography. As commercial agents if you are selling or leasing commercial property, there is now a cost effective way to produce stunning aerial photographs and videos of offices, retail parks, industrial units, factories, farms and land.

Sport, Travel and Tourism

Drone Aerial Photography and videography using unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV’s) can now really show the sports, leisure, travel and tourism industries from an exciting and affordable viewpoint, previously only available from expensive helicopter or manned aircraft shoots.

Building Inspections & Construction Sites

Drone Photography Services are specialists in high-level aerial building, tower, chimney, roof surveys and inspections using radio controlled quadcopter drones UAV ’s. Construction site aerial photography can show every stage of a development from planning, foundations, building topping out through to completion. There is no better way of getting an overview of a construction site that by using aerial photography.
